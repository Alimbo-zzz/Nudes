Pose

Even though you encourage you to explore various creative ways of working with AiNude, be advised that certain poses tend to provide better results.

AiNude is based on a Neural Engine, so it learns with every photo submitted, but for now we recommend avoiding photos taken from behind and with crossed legs.

Clothes

For the best results we recommend using photos with visible legs and hands.

They can be covered with clothes, but AiNude would work better if it could recognise the shape or position of body limbs.

General rule: the more skin that is exposed, the better the result. However, we strongly encourage you to experiment and see what works best.
